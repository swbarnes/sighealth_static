Jason R. Mercer M.D.

Dr. Mercer is a Board Certified Family Physician trained to provide comprehensive healthcare through the entire spectrum of life, from newborn to the elderly.

Dr. Mercer has over 15 years of experience treating patients in a variety of settings. In the office, the hospital or in your home, Dr. Mercer can provide assessment and treatment for acute and chronic medical conditions. His practice includes office gynecology, skin lesion removals, cryotherapy of skin lesions, joint injections and a wide range of minor surgical procedures. As an established member of the medical community, Dr. Mercer has access to the full range of medical consultants who can provide specialty consultations when needed.

Dr. Mercer is a 1990 graduate of the University of Florida College of Medicine. In 1993, he completed residency in Family Medicine at Halifax Medical Center in Daytona Beach where he served as chief resident. Dr. Mercer has been in medical practice in the Daytona Beach area since that time. He has served as Medical Director of two local nursing and rehabilitation centers, and he is currently the Medical Director of the Flagler County Schools.

Besides enjoying time with his family, Dr. Mercer has an interest in International Medical Missions. He has been involved in combined Medical-Dental mission trips to Tanzania, Brazil, and Chile. He has also traveled to the Mississippi Gulf Coast to assist with disaster relief.
